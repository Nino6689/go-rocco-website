How We Prioritise

Our roadmap is guided by three principles:

  1. Safety First: Features that help prevent dog incidents take priority.
  2. Community Feedback: We listen to our early adopters. The features you ask for move up the list.
  3. Privacy by Design: Every feature is built with privacy in mind. We won't add something that compromises your data.
