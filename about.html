Our Story

Built by dog people, for dog people

It Started With a Walk Gone Wrong

Picture this: You're in the park with your dog. They're off-lead, having the time of their life. Then you spot another dog in the distance ‚Äî this one's on a lead. Your stomach drops.

Is that dog friendly? Nervous? Reactive? You start the awkward dance. Squinting. Waving. Trying to make eye contact. Shouting "IS HE FRIENDLY?" across the park while your dog bounds toward them at full speed.

Sometimes it's fine. Sometimes it's really not fine.

"We thought: what if you could just... know? Before you got close. Before anyone had to shout. Before things could go wrong."

That's the moment Go Rocco was born.

Why "Go Rocco"?

Rocco is a real dog. A beautiful, slightly chaotic mini dachshund who belongs to one of our founders. He's friendly to a fault ‚Äî the kind of dog who assumes every other dog wants to be his best friend (despite being about a tenth of their size).

But not every dog wants that. Some dogs are nervous. Some are reactive. Some are in training. And Rocco bounding over uninvited wasn't fair to them ‚Äî or their owners.

We built Go Rocco so owners like us could know before we meet. So Rocco can play with dogs who want to play, and give space to dogs who don't.
