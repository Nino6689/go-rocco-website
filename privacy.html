Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data

Last updated: 14 January 2026

1. Who We Are

Go Rocco ("we", "us", "our") is a dog safety application operated in the United Kingdom. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Data Controller: Go Rocco Ltd
Contact: info@gorocco.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

2.1 Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile photo (optional)

2.2 Dog Profile Information

  • Dog's name, breed, age, and photo
  • Temperament settings (friendly, nervous, reactive, in training, selective)
  • Health records and vaccination dates (Pet Passport feature)

2.3 Location Data

  • Real-time location during active walks (only when you choose to be visible)
  • Walk routes and history
  • Location is only shared with other Go Rocco users within 50 metres

2.4 Usage Data

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Core Service Delivery: To show your dog's temperament to nearby users and show you theirs (legitimate interest)
  • Safety Features: To enable walk check-in and trusted contact alerts (consent/contract)
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account (contract)
  • Service Improvement: To analyse usage patterns and improve the app (legitimate interest)
  • Communications: To send service updates and, with your consent, marketing messages

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide our services to you
  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent (e.g., marketing, location sharing)
  • Legitimate Interest: Where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, balanced against your rights
  • Legal Obligation: Where we are required to process data by law

5. Location Data and Privacy Controls

Your location privacy is paramount to us:

  • 50-Metre Rule: Your location is only visible to other users within 50 metres
  • Visibility Control: You choose when to be visible on the map
  • Mutual Blocking: If you block another user, neither of you can see each other
  • Dog Owners Only: Only registered dog owners with verified profiles can see the map
  • No Location History Sharing: We do not share your location history with other users

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Other Users: Your dog's profile and real-time location (when visible) with nearby users
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and payment processors who process data on our behalf under contract
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect safety

6.1 Third-Party Data Processors

We use the following third-party services to process your data:

  • Cloudflare (US): Website hosting, security, and database services. Data is processed in accordance with Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Resend (US): Email delivery for confirmations and marketing communications (with your consent). Data is processed in accordance with Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers. Resend Privacy Policy

6.2 Marketing Communications

When you sign up to our early adopter waitlist or beta program, we use a double opt-in process to ensure genuine consent:

  1. You submit your email address and explicitly agree to receive marketing updates
  2. We send a confirmation email to verify your email address
  3. You must click the confirmation link to complete your subscription

We record the date, time, and IP address of your consent for our records. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any of our emails, or by contacting us at info@gorocco.com.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Location data: Real-time only; not stored beyond the active session unless part of saved walk history
  • Walk history: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Analytics data: Anonymised and retained for up to 2 years
  • Waitlist/marketing data: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion. Consent records are retained for 3 years after unsubscription for legal compliance purposes

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@gorocco.com. We will respond within one month.

9. International Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed within the UK and European Economic Area. If we transfer data outside these regions, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

11. Children's Privacy

Go Rocco is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Go Rocco after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us first at info@gorocco.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

Information Commissioner's Office
Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire SK9 5AF
ico.org.uk
